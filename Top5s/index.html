My Top 5s

Here are my top 5 favorite...


Films

  1. Interstellar (2014) dir. Christopher Nolan

  2. Annihilation (2018) dir. Alex Garland

  3. Call Me by your Name (2018) dir. Luca Guadagnino

  4. The Incredibles (2004) dir. Brad Byrd
  5. Oldboy (2003) dir. Park Chan-wook


Video Games

  • Metroid Prime (2002)
    First-person shooter-adventure game originally released on Nintendo GameCube. Beautiful, immersive environments, gorgeous soundtrack, and a feeling of isolation like no other.

  • Elden Ring (2022)
    Perhaps the greatest open world game of all-time, easily one of the most engaging, beautiful, and polished experiences ever made.

  • Destiny 2 (2017)
    The game that I have the most amount of time spent in, and has perhaps influenced my life the most through the experiences I have had with it directly and indirectly.

  • Pikmin 2 (2004)
    My favorite game in one of my all-time favorite game franchises, one of the most genuinely unique video games I've ever played, filled with charm, and excellent gameplay.

  • Nier:Automata (2017)
    One of the greatest stories ever told in video games, with incredible characters, writing, world-buildling, and a wholly unique structure unlike anything else.
